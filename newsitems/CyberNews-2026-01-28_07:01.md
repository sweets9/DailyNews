With reports indicating that over 70% of successful breaches originate from endpoints like phones and laptops, companies are moving away from traditional network perimeter defense. This comes amid broader calls for proactive security measures, such as regular Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT), to identify and fix weaknesses before they can be exploited.
