**Your Company's Data "Shadows" and "Ghosts" Pose a Major Risk**

Security analysts are highlighting the growing dangers of "dark data" and "shadow AI." Dark data is all the information a company collects but doesn't manage, like old project files, forgotten spreadsheets, or unused logs. Shadow AI refers to employees using unapproved AI tools for work tasks. Both create massive security blind spots. This unmanaged data and unsanctioned software are not protected by standard security measures, making them easy targets for attackers. Experts predict that these unmanaged resources will become a leading cause of data breaches in the coming year.

Why it Matters: A breach of "dark data" can still lead to regulatory fines, reputational damage, and the exposure of sensitive information. "Shadow AI" can leak confidential company data to third-party AI models without any oversight. Businesses need to urgently get a handle on the data they have and control the software their employees use.
