**TrustLogix to Showcase AI Agent Security Platform**
TrustLogix, a company specializing in AI-powered data security, announced it will be demonstrating its new platform for securing AI agents at the upcoming Gartner Data & Analytics Summit. The platform is designed to provide better access controls and data security for AI operating in enterprise networks.

Why it Matters: As more companies use AI, there is a growing risk of data breaches and misuse of information by these AI systems. This technology aims to reduce that risk by controlling what data AI agents can access, which is crucial for protecting sensitive business information.
